### Note
- The population data is from 1920 to 2015, created from 2 sources of open data by Japanese Ministry of Internal Affairs and Communication
  - population_1920_2000: [excel](https://www.e-stat.go.jp/stat-search/file-download?statInfId=000000090264&fileKind=0)
  - population_2000_2015: [excel](https://www.e-stat.go.jp/stat-search/file-download?statInfId=000013168604&fileKind=0)
- The eldest age part of population is ommited from the data
  because they don't have specific age value(Ex. "age 90 or greater")
- In the survival curve, foreign in and outs are not taken into account.
